# How to Use the Passport Translation Pilot

**Messenginfo — Controlled Pilot**

---

## 1. What this service does

This tool helps you prepare an **English translation draft** of your Ukrainian passport data page.

- You will see each piece of information that was read from your document.
- **You must check every field carefully** and confirm it is correct.
- This is **not legal advice**.
- This is **not a certified translation** by an attorney.
- You are responsible for verifying that the translation matches your document before signing.

---

## 2. Before you start

To get the best results, prepare your passport and phone:

- Place the passport on a **flat, well-lit surface**
- Use **good lighting** — natural light or a bright room is best
- **No flash** — flash creates glare and makes text hard to read
- Include the **entire page** — do not crop the edges
- Make sure all **numbers, text, and stamps** are visible
- **Do not cover** the passport number, series, or any dates
- Take the photo straight on — **do not tilt** the camera

---

## 3. Step by step

**Step 1 — Upload your photo**
Tap "Upload" and choose a photo of your passport data page.

**Step 2 — Wait while the system reads your document**
This usually takes 10–30 seconds. Please do not close the page.

**Step 3 — Check each field**
You will see a list of information the system found:
- Your name
- Date of birth
- Place of birth
- Passport series and number
- Who issued the passport
- Date of issue

**Step 4 — Tap Correct if something is wrong**
If something is wrong, tap **Correct** and type the correct value from your document.

**Step 5 — Tap Confirm only if it matches your document**
Only tap **Confirm** after you have checked the information against the actual passport page.

**Step 6 — Sign the certification**
After all fields are confirmed, you will be asked to sign. This confirms that you reviewed the translation.

**Step 7 — Complete payment**
After signing, you will be taken to the payment page.

**Step 8 — Download your PDF**
After payment, your translation document will be ready. An operator will review it and send you a download link.

---

## 4. What to check carefully

These fields are the most important. Check them against your physical passport:

| Field | What to check |
|---|---|
| **Last name (surname)** | Spelling — Ukrainian names can be transliterated in different ways |
| **First name** | Spelling — same as above |
| **Date of birth** | Make sure day and month are not swapped |
| **Place of birth** | City and region should match your document |
| **Passport series** | Two Cyrillic letters |
| **Passport number** | Six digits |
| **Issuing authority** | The office that issued your passport |
| **Date of issue** | Make sure day and month are not swapped |

---

## 5. If something is wrong

If something is wrong, tap **Correct** and type the correct value from your document.

Do not confirm a field if it does not match your physical passport.

If you are not sure what a field should say, look at the passport page directly and copy the information exactly as written.

---

## 6. Important notice

This tool does not provide legal advice. You are responsible for checking that the translation matches your document before signing.

This draft translation is prepared for your review. An operator will also check it before it is delivered to you.

This service is for **Ukrainian internal passports only**.
