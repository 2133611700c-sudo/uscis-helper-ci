## Extracted Content Structure

The Gemini extraction identified the following major sections:

### (A) Re-Parole RFE Scenario
- **Overview:** When I-131 (Re-Parole) is filed online with less than 500 symbols of explanation, USCIS may issue an RFE (Request For Evidence)
- **RFE Requirements:** Brief description of re-parole reason + submitted evidence + supporting documents in evidence attachments section
- **Cover Letter:** Required when submitting RFE response

### (B) Fee Waiver Strategy (I-912 + HR-1)
- **HR-1 Form:** Used to request refund of filing fees paid (includes I-131, I-765, I-821)
- **Strategy:** Fee waiver form I-912 + HR-1 refund request allows applicants to file without immediate payment or request reimbursement

### (C) Basic Filing Requirements
- **Government Benefits:** Most applicants have access to government benefits (SNAP, Medicaid, Food Stamps) making them eligible for fee waiver under 400% poverty line threshold
- **Medical Insurance Evidence:** Scans of medical insurance cards (both sides) required as benefit documentation for Verification of Benefits letter from Welfare department (required for fee waiver)

### (D) Common Mistakes & Prevention
- **Mistake #1 (Children):** I-912 can only be filed once per household, not once per child — one I-912 covers entire family even if benefits received by only one child
- **Name Change:** When changing surname/transliteration, must explicitly declare all previous variations (nickname, different, which uniqueness) and include supporting documents with department Welfare letter noting data changes and persons affected

### (E) Required Documents for Filing
- **Medical Insurance Evidence:** Scans of both sides of medical insurance card (official benefit verification from Welfare department required)
- **I-131 (Re-Parole) Filing:** Must be filed without supporting list + evidence must be necessary for I-821 purposes
- **RFE Response:** When USCIS requests evidence for re-parole I-131 without supportive list and evidence, must quickly describe brief reason in cover letter + attached evidence
