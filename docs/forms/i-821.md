# Form I-821 Intelligence

Updated: 2026-04-30

- Official page: https://www.uscis.gov/i-821
- Form PDF: https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/document/forms/i-821.pdf
- Instructions PDF: https://www.uscis.gov/sites/default/files/document/forms/i-821instr.pdf
- Edition date: 01/20/25
- Filing method: online or paper depending on TPS designation guidance

## Official Use

USCIS states Form I-821 is used to apply for Temporary Protected Status.

USCIS also states users may file Form I-765 with Form I-821 when requesting an EAD.

## Extractable from documents

- passport identity fields
- country of citizenship
- national ID if uploaded
- A-number for re-registration if visible

## Manual user inputs

- U.S. mailing address
- TPS eligibility-related facts
- criminal/arrest disclosures
- contact details
- any inadmissibility or waiver-related answers

## Filing tips pulled from official page

USCIS says it will reject missing key sections including Part 1, Part 2 family name, U.S. mailing address, A-number for re-registration, and date of birth.
